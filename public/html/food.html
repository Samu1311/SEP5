Food & Composting

The Food category advocates for conscious consumption practices that support sustainability, health, and ethical food choices. Engage in activities that promote responsible eating habits and a positive impact on the environment.

Sustainable Agriculture

Sustainable agriculture supports local farmers and reduces carbon footprints by sourcing produce from nearby markets or farm stores. Earn points for every purchase made from local farmers, contributing to the promotion of sustainable farming practices and supporting local communities.
