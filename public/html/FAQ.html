Account Related

1. How do I create an account?
To create an account, navigate to the "Sign Up" option in the right corner on the bar at the top. Fill in the required information such as username, email, and password to create your account.
2. How can I change my username/password?
You can change your username/password by accessing your account settings. Look for the "Change Username" or "Change Password" option and follow the instructions provided.
3. What should I do if I forgot my password?
If you forgot your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page in the right corner o the bar at the top. Follow the steps to reset your password using your registered email address.
4. I haven't received a confirmation email after registering. What should I do?
Check your spam or junk folder. If you still haven't received it, try registering again. Contact support if the issue persists.
