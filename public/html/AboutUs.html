How the Idea Was Born

The idea of EcoHero was born out of a desire to address the growing concern over environmental issues. The team, consisting of four university students from Via University College Denmark, was tired of hearing about the problems facing our planet and the lack of action being taken to address them. They realized that many people want to help but don’t know where to start or feel overwhelmed by the scale of the problem. That’s how EcoHero began - as a way to help people find motivation for helping the planet by giving them small, achievable tasks that would encourage them to continue. The team, a combination of environmental fanatics and software developers, worked tirelessly to bring the EcoHero of today to life.

Our Mission

At EcoHero, our mission is to empower people to take action and make a positive impact on the environment. We believe that small, achievable tasks can make a big difference, and we’re here to help you find motivation and inspiration to make a change. Our platform is designed to help you track your progress and see the impact you’re making. We’re inspired by the UNESCO 17 goals, and we’re committed to making a difference in the world.
